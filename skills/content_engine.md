# Skill: Content Engine

## Purpose
Generate conversion-focused content for active campaigns.

## Inputs
- Campaign name
- ICP
- Emotional angle or story
- Content types needed
- Platform

## Process
1. Identify the ICP's primary pain and aspiration
2. Select hook format that matches the tension
3. Build content around one idea — not multiple
4. End every piece with a clear CTA

## Outputs
- Hooks
- Reel scripts
- Captions
- Story sequences
- Short-form landing page copy

## Rules
- One idea per piece
- No filler, no padding
- Every piece must connect to the offer or a conversation
- Voice must match campaign (personal for retreat, ROI-focused for B2B)

## Success Metric
- DMs generated from content
- Applications driven from specific pieces
- Save and share rate (authority content)
