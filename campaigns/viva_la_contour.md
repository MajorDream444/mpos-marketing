# Campaign: Viva La Contour

## Status
Queued — activate after retreat campaign structure is running

## Goal
Sell first body contouring machine to a med spa, aesthetic clinic, or body contouring business.

## Offer
Viva La Contour machine
- Body contouring / aesthetic treatment device
- High ROI for clinics (charge $200–600 per session)
- Low overhead per use

## ICP (Ideal Client)
- Med spa owner or aesthetic clinic operator
- Already offering body treatments (CoolSculpting, cavitation, etc.)
- Looking to expand services or replace outdated equipment
- Instagram active, Google Maps listed
- Revenue: $200k–$2M+ annually

## Hook Angles (B2B)
1. "Your clients are asking for body contouring. Are you offering it?"
2. "One machine. $400/session. 3 clients/day. Do the math."
3. "The med spas adding $20k/month aren't doing more — they're offering more."
4. "You already have the clients. Now give them the service they're Googling."
5. "Stop referring out body contouring. Start owning it."

## Outreach Strategy
- Direct DM to med spa IG accounts
- Cold outreach via email (scraped from Google Maps)
- WhatsApp follow-up for warm responses

## Conversion Path
Scout lead → DM opener → Qualify (do they offer body treatments?) → Send info → Book demo call → Close

## Agent Assignments
- Scout: build list of 50 med spas / clinics (IG + location)
- Storyteller: 5 outreach messages, 2 follow-up sequences, 1 short pitch deck paragraph
- Distributor: B2B content cadence (2x/week professional posts)
- Closer: manage DM responses, qualify, route to call

## Qualification Questions
1. "Are you currently offering any body contouring treatments?"
2. "Are you looking to expand your service menu this year?"
3. "Do you have the space and staff to add a new treatment?"

## Metrics
- Med spas contacted
- DM response rate
- Demo calls booked
- Machine sold
