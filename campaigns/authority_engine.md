# Campaign: Authority Engine

## Status
Active (runs in parallel with other campaigns)

## Goal
Position MAJOR PRIME OS and the operator as the definitive system for high-performers who are winning but unstructured.

## Why This Campaign Exists
Retreats and products close faster when the audience already sees you as the authority. This campaign builds the shelf — so when someone is ready to buy, you're already the obvious choice.

## ICP
- High-performers watching from the sidelines
- People who have seen your content before but haven't acted yet
- Potential retreat clients, OS install clients, referral partners

## Content Themes
1. **System Thinking** — "most people optimize effort, not structure"
2. **Operator Insights** — behind the scenes of building MAJOR PRIME OS
3. **Diagnostic Content** — "5 signs your business runs you instead of you running it"
4. **Results Without Explanation** — show outcomes, let people ask how
5. **Contrast Posts** — "what most people do vs what operators do"

## Content Schedule
- 2 authority posts per week (IG feed or LinkedIn)
- 1 longer-form breakdown per week (carousel or thread)
- Daily story touches (poll, question, or observation)

## Rules
- Never explain your whole system for free
- Always end with a path to work together
- Speak to the person who is already achieving — not the beginner
- Avoid motivational content that doesn't lead anywhere

## Agent Assignments
- Storyteller: 2 authority pieces + 1 breakdown per week
- Distributor: consistent schedule, never miss
- Closer: respond to anyone who engages with questions or insight comments

## Metrics
- Profile visit rate
- Save and share rate (indicates resonance)
- DMs generated from authority content
- Inbound inquiries mentioning content
