## Weekly Review Questions

1. What was the best-performing content piece this week? Why did it work?
2. What was the lowest-converting DM sequence? What needs to change?
3. How many leads moved from Yellow to Green?
4. What campaign needs more attention next week?
5. What should we stop doing?

---

## Weekly Targets by Campaign Phase

### Pre-Launch (6+ weeks out)
- 10+ new leads per week
- 3 authority content pieces
- 20 DMs sent
- 5 conversations active

### Active Campaign (4 weeks out)
- 20+ new leads per week
- 5 content pieces
- 30–40 DMs sent
- 10+ conversations active
- 2+ applications per week

### Final Push (1–2 weeks out)
- Maximum volume
- Daily story pushes
- All follow-ups running
- Every warm lead contacted
- Applications reviewed daily
