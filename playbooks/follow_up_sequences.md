# Follow-Up Sequences

## Purpose
Structured follow-up logic so no warm lead goes cold due to lack of follow-through.

## Rules
- Never follow up more than 3 times without a reply
- Always give them an easy way to say no
- The third message closes the loop — don't leave it open forever
- Change the angle with each follow-up — don't repeat the same message

---

## Sequence A: Application Not Submitted (sent link, no action)

**Day 2:**
"Hey — just checking in. Did the application link work okay? Happy to answer any questions before you submit."

**Day 5:**
"Wanted to make sure this didn't get lost. The May cohort is filling — if you're still thinking about it, now's the time."

**Day 8:**
"Last follow-up on this — don't want to keep pinging if the timing's off. Here's the link if you decide you want in: [LINK]. Otherwise no worries — I'll keep you in mind for the next one."

---

## Sequence B: Warm Lead Gone Quiet (was engaged, stopped responding)

**Day 3:**
"Hey — noticed we lost the thread. Still relevant for you or has something changed?"

**Day 7:**
"No pressure at all. Just didn't want to assume one way or the other. Still open if you want to pick up where we left off."

**Day 12:**
"Closing the loop on my end. If May doesn't work, I'll reach out when the next one opens. Take care."

---

## Sequence C: Yellow Lead — Nurture (not ready now)

**Week 2:**
"Hey — you mentioned timing was the thing. Just wanted to stay in touch. How's [thing they mentioned] going?"

**Week 4:**
"Still have a few spots for May — thought of you. Has anything shifted since we last talked?"

**Week 6:**
"Applications close April 25. If the timing has opened up, let me know and I'll prioritize your application."

---

## Sequence D: WhatsApp Broadcast Non-Responders

**Day 3 after broadcast:**
"Hey [Name] — sent a message a few days ago. Just making sure it came through. Worth a reply even if it's a no!"

**Day 7:**
"Last one — if the May retreat isn't the right fit I completely understand. But if you've been thinking about it, the application link is here: [LINK]"

---

## Tracking
Log all follow-ups in the weekly metrics sheet with:
- Lead name
- Sequence type
- Last message sent
- Date
- Response (Y/N)
- Status change (if any)
