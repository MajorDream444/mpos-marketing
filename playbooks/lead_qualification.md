# Lead Qualification Playbook

## Purpose
Standardize how MPOS Marketing assesses and categorizes leads so Closer knows exactly what to do with each conversation.

## The Three Buckets

### GREEN — Qualified
Send application link immediately. If they hesitate, book a call.

Criteria:
- Has active income or business
- Feels the pain that the offer solves
- Has expressed desire to change
- Has responded more than once
- Has asked about price, timeline, or next steps

---

### YELLOW — Nurture
Move to follow-up sequence. Stay warm. Revisit in 7–14 days.

Criteria:
- Interested but not urgent
- Has income but not sure about timing
- Needs more context or social proof
- Engaging but not committing

---

### RED — Disqualify
Close warmly. Do not invest more time.

Criteria:
- No income, no business, no budget signal
- Just curious or browsing
- Wants free advice
- Has not responded to 2 follow-ups

---

## Qualification Questions (Natural Language)

These are not a script — they're directional. Work them into the conversation naturally.

1. "What does your work / business look like right now?"
2. "What's the main thing you feel like is missing?"
3. "Have you invested in anything like this before?"
4. "Is this something you're looking to act on now or more thinking about it?"

---

## Scoring Logic

| Signal | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Has active income | +2 |
| Has clear pain | +2 |
| Has asked about price | +3 |
| Has asked about dates/timing | +2 |
| Has responded 3+ times | +1 |
| Has referred someone | +3 |
| Has not responded in 5+ days | -2 |
| Said "not now" more than once | -3 |

**8+ = Green. 4–7 = Yellow. Under 4 = Red.**

---

## Hand-Off Protocol
When a lead reaches Green status:
1. Send application link
2. Flag lead in tracking sheet as "Application Sent"
3. Escalate to Captain if they submit application or ask for a call
4. Operator (you) takes over from call stage forward
