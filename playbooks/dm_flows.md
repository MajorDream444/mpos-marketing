# DM Flow Playbook

## Purpose
Scripts and logic for Instagram DMs — outreach, inbound management, and qualification.

## Rules
- Never open with a pitch
- One question at a time
- Mirror their energy
- Get them talking before presenting anything
- Always end with a clear next step

---

## Outbound DM Flows

### Flow 1: Content Engagement Opener
Use when: Someone liked a post, watched a reel, or saved something.

```
Message 1:
"Hey [Name] — saw you engaged with that post. Hit different for you or just stumbled across it?"

Wait for reply.

Message 2 (if they engage):
"Makes sense. What's your situation like right now — are you in that phase?"

Message 3 (if they're relevant):
"I'm running something in May specifically for [ICP description]. Not sure if the timing works but want to make sure you knew about it."

Message 4 (CTA):
"Want me to send you the details?"
```

---

### Flow 2: Cold-Warm Outreach (Scout-sourced lead)
Use when: Scout surfaced a high-fit account.

```
Message 1:
"Hey [Name] — came across your profile. You're clearly building something real."

Wait for reply.

Message 2:
"I work with [ICP]. What you're doing looks like it fits that exactly. What does your setup look like behind the scenes?"

Message 3 (after reply):
"Got it. I'm running a [offer] in May — sounds like it could be relevant. Want to hear more?"
```

---

## Inbound DM Flows

### Flow: Someone asks about the retreat
```
Message 1:
"Glad it caught your attention. Tell me a bit about where you're at right now — what does your [business / life / work] look like?"

After reply — qualify:
Message 2:
"And what's the main thing you feel like you're missing or want to fix?"

After reply — decide:
- Green: "This sounds like a fit. Here's the application: [LINK]"
- Yellow: "I want to make sure it's the right move for you — can we do a quick 15 min call?"
- Red: "Appreciate you sharing. I don't think this specific thing is the right fit but I'll keep you in mind."
```

---

## Qualification Decision Tree

```
Do they have income or a business?
→ No → Nurture or disqualify
→ Yes → Continue

Do they have a clear pain that matches the offer?
→ No → Explore more
→ Yes → Continue

Are they ready to invest / act now or soon?
→ No → Follow-up sequence
→ Yes → Send application or book call
```

---

## Follow-Up Sequence (DM)

**Day 2 after no reply:**
"Just wanted to make sure this didn't get buried — still relevant for you?"

**Day 5:**
"Last nudge on this one. Here's the link if you want to take a look: [LINK]"

**Day 10:**
"No worries if the timing's off. I'll keep you in mind for the next one."
