# Instagram Reels Playbook

## Purpose
Create short-form video content that stops the scroll, builds authority, and drives inbound DMs or profile visits.

## Reel Structure (30–60 seconds)

### Format
1. **Hook** (0–3 sec): Text on screen + spoken line. Must stop scroll.
2. **Problem/Tension** (3–15 sec): Name the pain or contrast the identity.
3. **Insight or Shift** (15–40 sec): The turn. What they don't know, what changes.
4. **CTA** (40–60 sec): One clear action. "DM me X" or "Link in bio."

---

## Reel Script Template

```
HOOK:
[TEXT ON SCREEN — 5 words or less]
[SPOKEN]: "[First sentence that matches or expands the hook]"

PROBLEM:
"[Name the pain they're in right now — be specific]"

INSIGHT:
"[What you know that they don't. One clear shift.]"

CTA:
"[If you're ready to [desired outcome], [specific action].]"
```

## Example Scripts

### Script 1: The Burnout Reframe
**Hook (text):** You're not burned out.
**Spoken:** "You're not burned out. You're unstructured. And there's a difference."
**Problem:** "Burnout comes from chaos — no clear systems, no operating rhythm, just constant output with no machine underneath."
**Insight:** "When you install the system, the same amount of work produces twice the result. And you stop feeling like you're drowning."
**CTA:** "If you're a high-performer who's tired of winning on fumes — DM me 'SYSTEM' and let's talk."

---

### Script 2: The Scale Problem
**Hook (text):** You can't scale chaos.
**Spoken:** "You can't scale chaos. But most people try."
**Problem:** "They add more offers, more content, more clients — and wonder why everything feels heavier."
**Insight:** "The business doesn't need more. It needs structure. One clear operating system and everything else gets easier."
**CTA:** "If your business runs you instead of the other way around — link in bio."

---

### Script 3: Retreat Specific
**Hook (text):** This isn't a spa retreat.
**Spoken:** "This is not a spa retreat. It's a working retreat for women who are already winning."
**Problem:** "You're making money. You're moving. But there's no system underneath — and you feel it."
**Insight:** "5 days. We build the structure together. You leave with an operating system, not just inspiration."
**CTA:** "Applications for May are open. Link in bio. Limited seats."

## Production Notes
- No talking head only — use text overlay + B-roll or movement
- Captions always on
- First frame must be visually interesting
- Music: low, not distracting
- Length: 30–45 sec converts better than 60 sec for authority content

## Posting Rules
- 3–5 reels per week during active campaign
- Always include DM CTA or bio link CTA
- Reply to every comment within 2 hours of posting
