# Hook Library

Hooks are the most important sentence you write. If the hook fails, nothing else matters.

## Hook Formula
[Pattern interrupt] + [Identity trigger] + [Tension or curiosity]

---

## Category: Burnout / Overextension

- "You're not tired. You're unstructured."
- "Burnout is a systems problem, not a mindset problem."
- "The reason you're exhausted isn't the workload. It's the chaos underneath it."
- "Working harder isn't the problem. The machine is broken."
- "You built the income. Now you have to build the system or lose the income."

---

## Category: Identity / Aspiration

- "High performers don't need motivation. They need infrastructure."
- "You're already winning. The question is: for how long without a system?"
- "Athletes don't wing it. Neither should your business."
- "The most impressive people I've met don't have more energy — they have better structure."
- "You don't need a rebrand. You need a rebuild."

---

## Category: Contrast / Pattern Interrupt

- "Most people are optimizing effort. Operators optimize structure."
- "Everyone's talking about content. Nobody's talking about the system behind it."
- "Stop building the audience. Start building the machine."
- "It's not that you lack discipline. It's that you lack design."
- "A busy calendar is not a business. It's a symptom."

---

## Category: Direct / Provocative

- "If you stopped tomorrow, what keeps running?"
- "You can't scale chaos."
- "Your systems are either working for you or against you. There is no neutral."
- "Every business problem is a structure problem in disguise."
- "The best time to build the system was before you needed it."

---

## Category: Retreat Specific

- "5 days. One system. Everything changes."
- "The retreat isn't for people who are struggling. It's for people who are winning and tired of it feeling that way."
- "You built the income. Now build the life around it."
- "What happens when high-performing women stop performing for 5 days and start building?"
- "This is not a spa retreat. It's a system install."

---

## Hook Testing Rules
- Test 3 hooks per campaign per week
- Track saves, shares, and DMs generated
- Keep what converts. Kill what doesn't.
- Never reuse a dead hook — rewrite it.
